Drop Dumplings



Nutrition : 181 calories,
Prep Time : PT22M
Cook Time : PT1H
4.2 stars - based on 103 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 2 c flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1 2 tsp salt
  • 3 4 c milk

Directions

  • Combine until just moistened
  • Drop by spoonfuls onto bubbling stew chicken and gravy etc
  • Cover and simmer 12 minutes
