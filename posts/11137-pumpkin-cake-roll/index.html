Pumpkin Cake Roll



Nutrition : 190 calories,
Prep Time : PT35M
Cook Time : PT1H
4 stars - based on 132 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 c sugar
  • 2 3 c pumpkin
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 3 4 c flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 2 tsp salt
  • 1 2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 c finely chopped walnuts
  • powdered sugar

Directions

  • Beat eggs on high speed for 5 minutes
  • Gradually beat in sugar
  • Stir in pumpkin and lemon juice
  • Stir together flour salt spices and baking powder
  • Add to pumpkin mixture
  • Spread into greased and floured cookie sheet
  • Top with nuts
  • Bake at 375 u00b0 for 15 minutes
  • Turn out on towel sprinkled with powdered sugar
  • Roll towel and cake together
  • Cool for 1 hour
