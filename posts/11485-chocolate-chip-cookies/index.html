Chocolate Chip Cookies



Nutrition : 290 calories,
Prep Time : PT44M
Cook Time : PT1H
4.2 stars - based on 214 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients

  • 1 1 2 sticks real butter
  • 1 c brown sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 4 tsp real vanilla
  • 1 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 2 tsp salt
  • 2 c plus 2 Tbsp flour
  • 2 c chocolate chips

Directions

  • Slowly melt butter being careful not to burn
  • Add brown sugar while butter is still warm
  • If brown sugar is clumpy place with butter in microwave until sugar softens
  • Add eggs and vanilla and beat until creamy and of a slightly lighter color
  • In separate bowl mix dry ingredients together and add to mixture
  • Fold in chips
  • Place 2 inch balls on ungreased sheet and bake at 325 u00b0 for about 12 to 15 minutes
  • Take out and cool for one minute
