Marinated Carrots



Nutrition : 159 calories,
Prep Time : PT36M
Cook Time : PT2H
5 stars - based on 387 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 2 lb carrots cut up and cooked for 5 minutes
  • 1 chopped pepper
  • 1 c chopped celery
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 can tomato soup undiluted
  • 1 2 c salad oil
  • 3 4 c sugar
  • 3 4 c vinegar
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tbsp mustard
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  • Bring to boil all the ingredients and pour over veggies Refrigerate until chilled
