Crab Gumbo



Nutrition : 209 calories,
Prep Time : PT45M
Cook Time : PT1H
4.2 stars - based on 61 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1 can 28 oz tomatoes
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • salt
  • 1 8 tsp hot pepper sauce
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 pkg 10 oz frozen okra sliced
  • 2 pkg 6 oz frozen Alaska Snow crab meat thawed drained or imitation crab
  • 2 2 3 c cooked rice without salt or fat

Directions

  • Heat oil in large kettle on medium heat
  • Add flour stirring constantly until dark brown but not burned
  • Add onion and garlic Cook until tender stirring occasionally
  • Gradually stir in undrained tomatoes
  • Add sugar worcestershire sauce salt hot pepper sauce and bay leaf
  • Stir to break up tomatoes
  • Bring to a boil
  • Reduce heat
  • Cover
  • Simmer 10 minutes to blend flavor stirring occasionally
  • Add okra
  • Cook 5 minutes or until tender stirring occasionally
  • Add crab
  • Stir gently and heat through Discard bay leaf
  • Serve gumbo over rice
  • 4 servings
